---

Afternoon snack attacks are usually one of two things:

1. **True hunger** — protein/fat will fix it
2. **Sugar withdrawal / habit** — your body is asking for the dopamine hit it got at lunch

If option #2 sounds like you, here’s my go-to swap:

**The ACV Spritz:**
- 1 scoop ACV powder (or 1 tbsp liquid ACV)
- 8–10 oz water
- Squeeze of lemon (optional)
- A few drops of stevia or monk fruit (optional)

Drink it 15 minutes before you normally snack.

Most people tell me the craving just… disappears. Or drops enough that an apple or some nuts is enough.
