### Features Section
Section label: Features
Heading: Your Inbox, But Better
Subhead: Email Assistant plugs into your Gmail and handles the busywork so you can focus on the work that actually makes you money.

Feature 1 — Auto-Responds Instantly
Customer emails get answered in seconds — not hours. Inquiries, complaints, scheduling requests all handled automatically.

Feature 2 — Sounds Like You
Configure the tone — professional, friendly, or casual. We train it on your business so customers can't tell it's AI.

Feature 3 — You Stay in Control
Every email is logged. Flag sensitive ones for review. You can disable anytime. Your data never trains public models.

Weekly report: X emails handled, Y hours saved, Z new leads captured. Know exactly what your assistant did for you.
