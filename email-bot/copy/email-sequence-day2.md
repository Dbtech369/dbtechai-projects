**Subject:** When to take cinnamon, turmeric, ginger and ACV

---

Timing matters more than most people think.

Here’s what the data suggests:

- **Cinnamon extract** → with breakfast. Helps blunt the glucose spike from that first meal.
- **Turmeric (curcumin)** → with a meal containing fat. BioPerine® or black pepper helps absorption.
- **Ginger** → 15 minutes before meals. Supports digestion and movement.
- **ACV powder** → diluted in water, before larger meals. Helps with appetite and satiety.

**Important:** Don’t just dump everything into one capsule and hope. The order and pairing matters.
